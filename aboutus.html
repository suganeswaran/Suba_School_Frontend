With great pleasure I would like to inform you that our educational institution has been established for the ultimate purpose of doing service in and around Nerinjipettai In a rapidly changing world we. The suba memorial & educational trust need to take a fresh look at the improvement of our school. The challenge is to make the concepts for the betterment of children education.


Some of the common objectives are formulated by our school



  • To bring out inherent talents & skills of our children 2. To develop all-around personalities in our children
  • To import value based education and prepare our children to have a better future
  • To encourage our students to take active part in all games, sports and all other activities.
  • To insist parents for taking more interest on their children in keeping up self discipline so as to gain high intellectual achievements and also maintain physical and mental fitness
  • To induce the students to think, to query to start self learning and to create an awareness of national awarene integration

I welcome constructive suggestions & comments of parents that would help us to import better standards. I am sure the students will get substantial benefits from our institution. I wish them all success and have a bright future

Our school has come into existence on 2nd June 2004. It was started mainly to provide English medium educational facilities for Nerinjipettai and near villages.

We are offering Hindi as special subject for the benefit of the children educational tours are arranged for the benefit of the children. In order to develop their aesthetic and psychological evolution the main motive of our school is not merely to trust subject matter in the students head but to make the shine in every walk of life.

We cherish with very hops that our school will grow steadily and will come in flying colours by the grace of god with the blessings and the support of the parents.
